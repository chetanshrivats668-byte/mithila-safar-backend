ℹ️ About Yatri Point

Yatri Point is a travel and stay booking platform focused on simple, fast, and reliable service across Bihar and the Mithila region.
Our Services: Bus booking, hotel booking, rapid car rides, and café discovery in one place.
Our Mission: Make regional travel easier with transparent pricing, quick bookings, and local partner support.
Coverage: Serving travelers across Madhubani, Darbhanga, Jaynagar, Patna, and nearby cities.
Support Promise: We aim to provide responsive customer help and a smoother booking experience for every journey.

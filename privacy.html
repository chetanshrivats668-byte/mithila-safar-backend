🔒 Privacy Policy

At Yatri Point, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect:
• Personal information (name, email, phone number)
• Booking details (travel dates, destination, seat selection)
• Payment information (UPI ID, transaction details)
• Location data for delivery/pickup services
2. How We Use Your Information:
• To process and manage your bookings
• To communicate with you about your orders
• To provide customer support
• To improve our services and user experience
• To comply with legal obligations
3. Data Protection:
• Your personal data is encrypted and stored securely
• We use industry-standard security measures
• Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
• We never share your data with third parties without your consent
4. Payment Security:
• All UPI and payment transactions are processed through secure gateways
• We do not store your complete payment credentials
• Transaction history is encrypted for your protection
5. User Rights:
• You can access and update your personal information anytime
• You can request deletion of your account and data
• You have the right to opt-out of promotional communications
• Contact us to exercise these rights
6. Third-Party Services:
• We may share data with service collaborators (bus operators, hotels, cab drivers) as necessary for booking fulfillment
• Payment processors comply with their own privacy policies
• We are not responsible for third-party data practices
7. Children's Privacy:
• Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age
• We do not knowingly collect data from children
8. Changes to Policy:

• Any changes will be posted on this page
• Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance
